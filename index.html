About Us

Vibe Check began with the simple hypothesis that quick service and fast casual restaurants could increase their throughput by harnessing big data to better understand customer behavior and employee performance.

To achieve these goals, we built a first of its kind tool to automatically capture occupancy based analytics visible in real time across hundreds or even thousands of restaurants, providing owners with a complete picture of customer activity in their restaurants only seconds after it occurs.

With data captured on-site, Vibe Check increases restaurant throughput by reducing wait times, streamlining the menu, and identifying the most efficient and productive employees. Most importantly, Vibe Check provides real time insight across a portfolio of restaurant locations, so as an owner you can ensure that your customers have a positive experience and will return in the future.
